## 🏢 About Brandley

**Brandley** is a full-service digital marketing agency built from the ground up as a real, operating business — not a simulation. Founded as part of the **Digital Egypt Pioneers Initiative (DEPI)**, sponsored by Egypt's **Ministry of Communications and Information Technology (MCIT)**, Brandley was created by a team of five digital marketing specialists with a single goal: deliver measurable growth to businesses through strategy-driven marketing, creative execution, and data-informed decisions.

From day one, we operated as a professional agency — onboarding real clients, managing live ad budgets, producing original brand identities, and building social media presences from scratch. Every deliverable in this repository represents real work, real campaigns, and real results.

<br/>

| 🎯 Mission | 🔭 Vision |

| Help businesses establish powerful digital presences through creative strategy, performance marketing, and brand development. | Become Egypt's most trusted growth marketing partner for emerging businesses. |

### 🔥 Case Study 01 — Safety Group

<div align="center">

![Industry](https://img.shields.io/badge/Industry-Fire%20Protection%20Systems-red?style=flat-square)
![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/Campaign%20Status-Completed-brightgreen?style=flat-square)
![Started From](https://img.shields.io/badge/Started%20From-Zero-orange?style=flat-square)

</div>

**Safety Group** is an Egyptian company specializing in the supply and installation of fire protection systems and firefighting equipment. When they approached Brandley, they had no digital presence whatsoever — no social media page, no brand assets, and no audience. We built everything from the ground up.

<details>
<summary><b>📋 Scope of Work</b></summary>

<br/>

| Responsibility | Delivered |
|---|---|
| Facebook Page Creation | ✅ |
| Brand Identity | ✅ |
| Visual Identity System | ✅ |
| Content Strategy | ✅ |
| Content Planning | ✅ |
| Graphic Design | ✅ |
| Social Media Management | ✅ |
| Meta Ads Campaign | ✅ |
| Marketing Consultation | ✅ |

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>📊 Campaign Results</b></summary>

<br/>

<div align="center">

| Metric | Result |
|---|---|
| 👍 Page Likes | **783** |
| 👁️ Reach | **12,881** |
| 📢 Impressions | **21,677** |
| 💰 Ad Spend | **EGP 1,403.51** |
| 💡 Cost Per Like | **EGP 1.79** |

</div>

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>🏆 Achievement</b></summary>

<br/>

Safety Group arrived with no digital footprint. Brandley architected their entire online presence — from brand naming conventions and logo design to a fully operational Facebook page with an active audience. Within the campaign window, we achieved **783 page likes** at a cost-per-like of **EGP 1.79**, reaching over **12,000 unique users** and generating more than **21,000 impressions** — all on a highly controlled budget. This project demonstrated Brandley's ability to build a credible digital brand from absolute zero and deliver audience growth that sets the foundation for future paid performance at scale.

</details>

### 📚 Case Study 02 — Munazzem

<div align="center">

![Industry](https://img.shields.io/badge/Industry-EdTech-blue?style=flat-square)
![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/Campaign%20Status-Completed-brightgreen?style=flat-square)
![Type](https://img.shields.io/badge/Type-Pre--Launch%20Identity-purple?style=flat-square)

</div>

**Munazzem** is an educational management platform built to help teachers manage the full administrative cycle of their classrooms — including student records, attendance, grades, parent communication, financial tracking, assistant coordination, and scheduling. Brandley was brought in to develop the brand's complete digital identity before the product launched publicly.

<details>
<summary><b>📋 Scope of Work</b></summary>

<br/>

| Responsibility | Delivered |
|---|---|
| Brand Identity | ✅ |
| Logo Design | ✅ |
| Facebook Page Setup | ✅ |
| Social Media Design System | ✅ |
| Content Strategy | ✅ |
| Content Planning | ✅ |
| Marketing Consultation | ✅ |
| Original Brand Mascot (3D Character) | ✅ |

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>🤖 Brand Mascot</b></summary>

<br/>

As part of the Munazzem identity project, Brandley conceptualized and produced an **original 3D brand mascot** — a fully realized digital character designed to serve as the visual face of the brand across all marketing touchpoints. The mascot was developed entirely in-house as an original creation, giving Munazzem a distinctive, memorable presence that sets it apart from competing EdTech platforms. The character embodies the brand's values of organization, approachability, and intelligence — making complex classroom management feel human and engaging.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>🏆 Achievement</b></summary>

<br/>

Munazzem required a complete digital identity to be built before a single ad was served. Brandley delivered a cohesive brand system — logo, color palette, typography, page design, content frameworks, and an original 3D mascot — that positions the product for a high-impact launch. With the full identity architecture in place, Munazzem can enter paid advertising with a professional, trustworthy brand that converts. This engagement demonstrates Brandley's ability to function as a full creative and strategic partner for pre-launch products.

</details>

We help businesses grow by building brands people trust and running campaigns that deliver measurable results. Our work sits at the intersection of creative strategy and performance marketing — combining the storytelling power of great branding with the precision of data-driven advertising.

## 🔄 Our Workflow

<div align="center">

```
DISCOVERY → STRATEGY → CREATIVE → EXECUTION → OPTIMIZATION → REPORTING
```

</div>

| Phase | What We Do |
|---|---|

| **04 · Execution** | Page management, content publishing, ad campaign launch, community management |

Every phase is documented and delivered to the client. Nothing at Brandley is a black box.

## 🧠 Skills Applied

<details>
<summary><b>📈 Marketing Strategy</b></summary>

<br/>

Developed custom go-to-market strategies for each client — including audience segmentation, platform selection, campaign phasing, and funnel mapping. Every strategy was tailored to the client's industry, budget, and growth stage.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>📅 Content Strategy</b></summary>

<br/>

Built monthly content calendars that balance brand awareness, engagement, and conversion content across post formats. Content pillars were defined per client to ensure every post served a clear strategic purpose.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>🎨 Branding</b></summary>

<br/>

Delivered complete brand identity systems — including logo design, color palette, typography, visual tone, and brand guidelines — for multiple clients from scratch. Branding work ranged from safety industry to EdTech to retail.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>💸 Media Buying</b></summary>

<br/>

Managed live Meta Ads campaigns with real budgets, including audience targeting, objective selection, creative testing, bid strategy, and budget pacing. Demonstrated ability to deliver results on constrained daily budgets while optimizing cost-per-result.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>✍️ Copywriting</b></summary>

<br/>

Wrote all client-facing copy — captions, headlines, ad body text, and page descriptions — in Arabic. Developed a distinct voice for each brand to maintain consistency across touchpoints.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>🖼️ Graphic Design</b></summary>

<br/>

Produced all visual content in-house using Canva and Adobe Photoshop — including post templates, brand kits, product showcases, and campaign creatives. Maintained visual consistency across each client's content library.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>⚡ Performance Marketing</b></summary>

<br/>

Optimized campaigns around quantifiable outcomes — page likes, reach, cost-per-result — using real ad spend data. Applied performance marketing principles including audience lookalikes, interest-based targeting, and creative iteration.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>🔍 Audience Research</b></summary>

<br/>

Mapped target audiences for each client based on industry, geography, demographics, and behavioral signals. Used Meta Audience Insights and competitive research to define targeting parameters before campaign launch.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>🗂️ Campaign Planning</b></summary>

<br/>

Structured campaigns with clear objectives, creative briefs, scheduling plans, and budget allocations before execution. Maintained documentation throughout each campaign lifecycle for performance review.

</details>
